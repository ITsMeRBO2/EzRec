Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

1

Launch EzRec

Open the application and select your recording preferences. Choose between screen recording, audio recording, or both simultaneously.

2

Configure Settings

Adjust quality settings, select recording area, choose your microphone, and set output format according to your needs.

3

Start Recording

Click the record button and start creating your content. Use hotkeys for quick control and stop when you're done.

Pro Tips

  • Use Ctrl+Shift+R to start/stop recording quickly
  • Test your microphone levels before important recordings
  • Choose MP4 format for best compatibility
  • Close unnecessary applications for better performance
